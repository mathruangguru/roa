Math Olympiad Practice Problems 6

Topic: Logic, Visuals & Order

Questions / Soal

Topic: Logic & Classification

1. Observe the pattern below. Which shape is missing?

Perhatikan pola di bawah ini. Bangun apa yang hilang?

2. Which shape is different from the others? (Odd one out)

Manakah bangun yang berbeda dari yang lain?

3. Box A has 12 balls. Box B has 6 balls. How many balls must be moved from Box A to Box B so that both boxes have the SAME number of balls?

Kotak A memiliki 12 bola. Kotak B memiliki 6 bola. Berapa bola yang harus dipindahkan dari Kotak A ke Kotak B agar kedua kotak memiliki jumlah bola yang SAMA?

4. How many triangles are there in the figure below?

Ada berapa banyak segitiga pada gambar di bawah ini?

5. Look at the balance scales. If one Square weighs 4 kg, what is the weight of one Triangle?

Perhatikan timbangan berikut. Jika berat satu Kotak adalah 4 kg, berapakah berat satu Segitiga?

6. Find the missing numbers in the pattern: 2, 5, 8, 11, ?, 17.

Temukan angka yang hilang pada pola: 2, 5, 8, 11, ?, 17.

7. The opposite faces of a standard die always add up to 7. If the number 5 is on the top face, what number is on the bottom face?

Sisi yang berlawanan pada dadu standar selalu berjumlah 7. Jika angka 5 ada di sisi atas, angka berapakah yang ada di sisi bawah?

8. Which of these does NOT belong in the group?
A. Car    B. Bicycle    C. Airplane    D. Bus

Manakah yang TIDAK termasuk dalam kelompok ini?
A. Mobil    B. Sepeda    C. Pesawat Terbang    D. Bus

9. Look at the logic grid. What shape should be in the empty box?

Perhatikan kotak logika berikut. Bangun apa yang seharusnya ada di kotak kosong?

10. If A = 2 and B = 3, what is the value of A + B + A?

Jika A = 2 dan B = 3, berapakah nilai dari A + B + A?

11. In a class, 10 students like Chocolate ice cream. 8 students like Vanilla ice cream. If 3 students like BOTH, how many students like Chocolate ONLY?

Di sebuah kelas, 10 siswa suka es krim Cokelat. 8 siswa suka es krim Vanila. Jika 3 siswa suka KEDUANYA, berapa siswa yang HANYA suka Cokelat?

12. Which of these words looks the SAME when looked at in a mirror?
A. CAT    B. MOM    C. DAD    D. BOB

Manakah kata di bawah ini yang terlihat SAMA persis ketika dilihat di cermin?
A. CAT    B. MOM    C. DAD    D. BOB

13. Box X is heavier than Box Y. Box Z is lighter than Box Y. Which box is the heaviest?

Kotak X lebih berat dari Kotak Y. Kotak Z lebih ringan dari Kotak Y. Kotak manakah yang paling berat?

14. Start with number 5. Add 3. If the number is more than 7, subtract 1. What is the final number?

Mulai dengan angka 5. Tambah 3. Jika angkanya lebih dari 7, kurangi 1. Berapakah angka akhirnya?

15. How many small squares (size 1x1) are needed to make a larger square with side length 3?

Berapa banyak persegi kecil (ukuran 1x1) yang dibutuhkan untuk membuat persegi besar dengan panjang sisi 3?

Topic: Position & Order (Posisi & Urutan)

16. In a queue to buy toys, Ben is 4th from the front. How many people are in front of Ben?

Dalam antrean membeli mainan, Ben ada di urutan ke-4 dari depan. Ada berapa orang di depan Ben?

17. Sarah is standing in a line. She is the 5th person from the front and the 6th person from the back. How many people are there in the line?

Sarah berdiri dalam barisan. Dia adalah orang ke-5 dari depan dan orang ke-6 dari belakang. Berapa banyak orang dalam barisan tersebut?

18. In a race of 10 cars, the Red car is 1st. The Blue car is last. How many cars are between the Red car and the Blue car?

Dalam balapan 10 mobil, mobil Merah juara 1. Mobil Biru juara terakhir. Ada berapa mobil di antara mobil Merah dan mobil Biru?

19. You are running in a race. You overtake the person in 2nd place. What place are you in now?

Kamu sedang berlomba lari. Kamu menyalip orang di posisi ke-2. Di posisi keberapakah kamu sekarang?

20. Five friends are holding hands in a line facing you: A - B - C - D - E. Who is to the left of C from your perspective?

Lima teman bergandengan tangan dalam satu barisan menghadap ke arahmu: A - B - C - D - E. Siapakah yang berada di sebelah kiri C (dari sudut pandangmu)?

21. There is a stack of books. Math is at the bottom. English is on top of Math. Science is on top of English. History is at the very top. Which book is immediately below History?

Ada tumpukan buku. Matematika paling bawah. Bahasa Inggris di atas Matematika. Sains di atas Bahasa Inggris. Sejarah paling atas. Buku apa yang tepat berada di bawah Sejarah?

22. Write the letters of the word "OLYMPIAD". What letter is 5th from the left?

Tulis huruf-huruf dari kata "OLYMPIAD". Huruf apakah yang berada di urutan ke-5 dari kiri?

23. If yesterday was Friday, what day will it be 3 days after tomorrow?

Jika kemarin adalah hari Jumat, hari apakah 3 hari setelah besok?

24. A building has 12 floors. Tom lives on the 4th floor. Jerry lives 5 floors above Tom. On which floor does Jerry live?

Sebuah gedung memiliki 12 lantai. Tom tinggal di lantai 4. Jerry tinggal 5 lantai di atas Tom. Di lantai berapakah Jerry tinggal?

25. 4 kids sit at a round table in clockwise order: A, B, C, D. Who is sitting opposite A?

4 anak duduk di meja bundar dengan urutan searah jarum jam: A, B, C, D. Siapakah yang duduk berhadapan dengan A?

26. On a number line, number 10 is to the right of number 5. Is number 3 to the left or right of number 5?

Pada garis bilangan, angka 10 ada di sebelah kanan angka 5. Apakah angka 3 ada di sebelah kiri atau kanan angka 5?

27. There are 5 cards in a row: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5. If you swap the card in the 1st position with the card in the last position, what is the order now?

Ada 5 kartu berjejer: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5. Jika kamu menukar kartu di posisi ke-1 dengan kartu di posisi terakhir, bagaimana urutannya sekarang?

28. There are 8 people ahead of Tim and 5 people behind Tim. How many people are in the queue?

Ada 8 orang di depan Tim dan 5 orang di belakang Tim. Berapa banyak orang dalam antrean itu?

29. Tree A is taller than Tree B. Tree C is shorter than Tree B. Order them from Shortest to Tallest.

Pohon A lebih tinggi dari Pohon B. Pohon C lebih pendek dari Pohon B. Urutkan dari yang Terpendek ke Tertinggi.

30. In a line of children, Ali is 10th from the left and Budi is 10th from the right. There are 4 children between them. What is the total number of children in the line? (Assume Ali is to the left of Budi)

Dalam barisan anak-anak, Ali urutan ke-10 dari kiri dan Budi urutan ke-10 dari kanan. Ada 4 anak di antara mereka. Berapa jumlah total anak dalam barisan tersebut? (Asumsikan Ali berada di sebelah kiri Budi)

Detailed Solutions / Pembahasan Detail

A. Logic & Classification

1. Answer: Square (Persegi)

Pattern: Circle, Triangle, Square. (Repeats). After Triangle, the next shape is Square.

Pola: Lingkaran, Segitiga, Persegi. (Berulang). Setelah Segitiga, bangun berikutnya adalah Persegi.

2. Answer: C (Rectangle/Persegi Panjang)

A, B, and D are circles (curved lines). C is a rectangle (straight lines).

A, B, dan D adalah lingkaran (garis lengkung). C adalah persegi panjang (garis lurus).

3. Answer: 3 Balls (3 Bola)

Concept: Transfer to Equalize.

Difference is $12 - 6 = 6$. To make them equal, move half of the difference. $6 \div 2 = 3$.

Selisihnya adalah $12 - 6 = 6$. Pindahkan setengah dari selisihnya. $6 \div 2 = 3$.

4. Answer: 2 Triangles

1. Small triangle at top. 2. Large outer triangle. The bottom shape is a trapezoid. Total = 2.

1. Segitiga kecil di atas. 2. Segitiga besar terluar. Bangun bawah adalah trapesium. Total = 2.

5. Answer: 4 kg

Scale 1: 2 Squares = 1 Circle (8kg). So Circle = 8kg.

Scale 2: 1 Circle (8kg) = 2 Triangles. So 1 Triangle = 4kg.

6. Answer: 14

Pattern is +3. $11 + 3 = 14$.

7. Answer: 2

Standard die rule: Opposite sides sum to 7. $7 - 5 = 2$.

8. Answer: C (Airplane)

Car, Bicycle, Bus are land transport. Airplane is air transport.

9. Answer: 2 Triangles ($\triangle\triangle$)

Logic: Double the shape. 1 Triangle becomes 2 Triangles.

10. Answer: 7

$A + B + A = 2 + 3 + 2 = 7$.

11. Answer: 7 Students

Chocolate ONLY = Total Chocolate - Both. $10 - 3 = 7$.

12. Answer: B (MOM)

"MOM" has vertical symmetry. D and B appear reversed in a mirror.

13. Answer: Box X

X > Y and Y > Z. So X is the heaviest.

14. Answer: 7

$5 + 3 = 8$. Is $8 > 7$? Yes. So, $8 - 1 = 7$.

15. Answer: 9

A square with side 3 is a $3 \times 3$ grid. Total = 9.

B. Position & Order

16. Answer: 3 people

$4 - 1 = 3$ people in front.

17. Answer: 10 people

Total = (Front Position + Back Position) - 1. $5 + 6 - 1 = 10$.

18. Answer: 8 cars

Cars between 1st and 10th = $10 - 1 - 1 = 8$.

19. Answer: 2nd Place

If you overtake the 2nd person, you take their position. You are now 2nd.

20. Answer: B

The line faces you: Left A - B - C - D - E Right. Left of C is B.

21. Answer: Science

Order (Bottom to Top): Math $\to$ English $\to$ Science $\to$ History.

22. Answer: P

O-L-Y-M-P-I-A-D.

23. Answer: Wednesday

Yesterday=Fri $\to$ Today=Sat $\to$ Tomorrow=Sun. 3 days after Sun = Wed.

24. Answer: 9th Floor

$4 + 5 = 9$.

25. Answer: C

Clockwise A, B, C, D. Opposite A (12 o'clock) is C (6 o'clock).

26. Answer: Left

$3 < 5$, so 3 is to the left.

27. Answer: 5, 2, 3, 4, 1

28. Answer: 14 people

$8 (\text{front}) + 1 (\text{Tim}) + 5 (\text{back}) = 14$.

29. Answer: C, B, A

C < B and B < A. So C, B, A.

30. Answer: 24 children

Separate groups logic: Left(10) + Between(4) + Right(10). Wait, Ali is 10th (9 people before him). Budi is 10th from right (9 people after him).

Structure: [9 people] - Ali - [4 people] - Budi - [9 people].

Total = $9 + 1 + 4 + 1 + 9 = 24$.

Logika kelompok terpisah: Kiri(10) + Antara(4) + Kanan(10) = 24.
