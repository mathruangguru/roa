(EN) Concept: Summing a repeating pattern.

(EN) Step 1: The pattern is "2, 4, 6". This pattern has 3 numbers.

(EN) Step 2: The sum of one pattern group is $2 + 4 + 6 = 12$.

(EN) Step 3: We need to find the sum of the first 42 numbers. We need to know how many times the pattern repeats. $42 \div 3 = 14$.

(EN) Step 4: The pattern repeats exactly 14 times. The total sum is $14 \times (\text{sum of one group}) = 14 \times 12$.
