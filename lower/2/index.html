Pembahasan / Solutions

Solutions Part 1: Basic Arithmetic

Solution 1 / Pembahasan 1:

Concept: Multi-step problem involving "more than" and "altogether".

Konsep: Soal multi-langkah melibatkan "lebih banyak dari" dan "jika digabungkan".

Solution:

  • Step 1: Find how many cars Budi has. Budi has 3 more than Ali's 7 cars. Budi has $7 + 3 = 10$ cars.
  • Step 2: Find the total number of cars. Add Ali's cars and Budi's cars. Total = $7 + 10 = 17$ cars.

Penyelesaian:

  • Langkah 1: Cari jumlah mobil Budi. Budi punya 3 lebih banyak dari 7 mobil Ali. Budi punya $7 + 3 = 10$ mobil.
  • Langkah 2: Cari jumlah total mobil. Tambahkan mobil Ali dan mobil Budi. Total = $7 + 10 = 17$ mobil.
Answer: They have 17 toy cars altogether. / Jawaban: Mereka memiliki 17 mobil mainan jika digabungkan.

Solution 2 / Pembahasan 2:

Concept: Multi-step problem involving subtraction, addition, and comparison.

Konsep: Soal multi-langkah melibatkan pengurangan, penjumlahan, dan perbandingan.

Solution:

  • Step 1: Find birds after 3 flew away. Start with 10. $10 - 3 = 7$ birds.
  • Step 2: Find birds after 2 more landed. Current is 7. $7 + 2 = 9$ birds.
  • Step 3: Compare the final number (9) to the start number (10). 9 is less than 10.
  • Step 4: Find the difference. $10 - 9 = 1$.

Penyelesaian:

  • Langkah 1: Cari jumlah burung setelah 3 terbang. Mulai dari 10. $10 - 3 = 7$ burung.
  • Langkah 2: Cari jumlah burung setelah 2 hinggap. Sekarang ada 7. $7 + 2 = 9$ burung.
  • Langkah 3: Bandingkan jumlah akhir (9) dengan jumlah awal (10). 9 lebih sedikit dari 10.
  • Langkah 4: Cari selisihnya. $10 - 9 = 1$.
Answer: There are fewer birds now. There is 1 fewer bird. / Jawaban: Jumlah burung sekarang lebih sedikit. Ada 1 burung lebih sedikit.

Solution 3 / Pembahasan 3:

Concept: Multi-step "missing part" problem involving addition first.

Konsep: Soal "bagian yang hilang" multi-langkah yang melibatkan penjumlahan terlebih dahulu.

Solution:

  • Step 1: Find how much money Siti has now. She started with 6 dollars and earned 2 more. $6 + 2 = 8$ dollars.
  • Step 2: Find how much more she still needs. The comic costs 10 dollars. She has 8 dollars. $10 - 8 = 2$ dollars.

Penyelesaian:

  • Langkah 1: Cari jumlah uang Siti sekarang. Dia mulai dengan 6 dolar dan mendapat 2 dolar lagi. $6 + 2 = 8$ dolar.
  • Langkah 2: Cari berapa banyak lagi yang masih dia butuhkan. Komik berharga 10 dolar. Dia punya 8 dolar. $10 - 8 = 2$ dolar.
Answer: She still needs 2 dollars more. / Jawaban: Dia masih membutuhkan 2 dolar lagi.

Solution 4 / Pembahasan 4:

Concept: Multi-step comparison problem involving "fewer than" and "more than".

Konsep: Soal perbandingan multi-langkah yang melibatkan "lebih sedikit dari" dan "lebih banyak dari".

Solution:

  • Step 1: Find how many marbles Rian has. Rian has 8 fewer than David's 15. $15 - 8 = 7$ marbles.
  • Step 2: Find how many marbles Siti has. Siti has 3 more than Rian's 7. $7 + 3 = 10$ marbles.

Penyelesaian:

  • Langkah 1: Cari jumlah kelereng Rian. Rian punya 8 lebih sedikit dari 15 kelereng David. $15 - 8 = 7$ kelereng.
  • Langkah 2: Cari jumlah kelereng Siti. Siti punya 3 lebih banyak dari 7 kelereng Rian. $7 + 3 = 10$ kelereng.
Answer: Siti has 10 marbles. / Jawaban: Siti memiliki 10 kelereng.

Solution 5 / Pembahasan 5:

Concept: Understanding tens and ones, combining multiple groups.

Konsep: Memahami puluhan dan satuan, menggabungkan beberapa kelompok.

Solution:

  • Step 1: Calculate dots in 2 full Ten Frames. Each has 10 dots. $10 + 10 = 20$ dots (or $2 \times 10 = 20$).
  • Step 2: Add the extra dots. There are 3 extra dots. $20 + 3 = 23$ dots.

Penyelesaian:

  • Langkah 1: Hitung titik dalam 2 Ten Frame penuh. Masing-masing punya 10 titik. $10 + 10 = 20$ titik (atau $2 \times 10 = 20$).
  • Langkah 2: Tambahkan titik ekstra. Ada 3 titik ekstra. $20 + 3 = 23$ titik.
Answer: There are 23 dots altogether. / Jawaban: Ada 23 titik seluruhnya.

Solution 6 / Pembahasan 6:

Concept: Using a Bar Model for multi-step problems (finding a part, then finding the whole).

Konsep: Menggunakan Bar Model untuk soal multi-langkah (mencari satu bagian, lalu mencari keseluruhan).

Solution:

  • Step 1: Find the value of Part 2. Part 1 is 9. Part 2 is 5 less than Part 1. Part 2 = $9 - 5 = 4$.
  • Step 2: Find the Total. The Total is Part 1 + Part 2. Total = $9 + 4 = 13$.

Penyelesaian:

  • Langkah 1: Cari nilai Bagian 2. Bagian 1 adalah 9. Bagian 2 adalah 5 lebih sedikit dari Bagian 1. Bagian 2 = $9 - 5 = 4$.
  • Langkah 2: Cari Totalnya. Total adalah Bagian 1 + Bagian 2. Total = $9 + 4 = 13$.
Answer: The Total is 13. / Jawaban: Totalnya adalah 13.

Solution 7 / Pembahasan 7:

Concept: Finding a missing addend in a multi-step addition.

Konsep: Mencari bilangan yang hilang dalam penjumlahan multi-langkah.

Solution:

  • Method 1 (Combine known parts first): $2 + 5 = 7$. The equation becomes $7 + ? = 15$. We need to find the difference between 15 and 7. $15 - 7 = 8$. So, $? = 8$.
  • Method 2 (Subtract known parts from total): Start with the total 15. Subtract the known parts. $15 - 2 = 13$. Then $13 - 5 = 8$. So, $? = 8$.

Penyelesaian:

  • Metode 1 (Gabungkan bagian yang diketahui dulu): $2 + 5 = 7$. Persamaannya menjadi $7 + ? = 15$. Kita perlu mencari selisih antara 15 dan 7. $15 - 7 = 8$. Jadi, $? = 8$.
  • Metode 2 (Kurangkan total dengan bagian yang diketahui): Mulai dengan total 15. Kurangkan bagian yang diketahui. $15 - 2 = 13$. Lalu $13 - 5 = 8$. Jadi, $? = 8$.
Answer: The missing number is 8. / Jawaban: Angka yang hilang adalah 8.

Solution 8 / Pembahasan 8:

Concept: Multi-step problem involving division (grouping) and subtraction.

Konsep: Soal multi-langkah yang melibatkan pembagian (pengelompokan) dan pengurangan.

Solution:

  • Step 1: Find the number of groups. There are 18 stickers, grouped into 3s. $18 \div 3 = 6$ groups.
  • Step 2: Find the number of stickers given away. 2 groups were given away. Each group has 3 stickers. $2 \times 3 = 6$ stickers given away.
  • Step 3: Find the number of stickers left. Started with 18, gave away 6. $18 - 6 = 12$ stickers left.

Penyelesaian:

  • Langkah 1: Cari jumlah grup. Ada 18 stiker, dikelompokkan per 3. $18 \div 3 = 6$ grup.
  • Langkah 2: Cari jumlah stiker yang diberikan. 2 grup diberikan. Setiap grup punya 3 stiker. $2 \times 3 = 6$ stiker diberikan.
  • Langkah 3: Cari jumlah stiker yang tersisa. Mulai dengan 18, diberikan 6. $18 - 6 = 12$ stiker tersisa.
Answer: I have 12 stickers left. / Jawaban: Aku punya sisa 12 stiker.

Solution 10 / Pembahasan 10:

Concept: Multi-step addition and subtraction tracking changes.

Konsep: Penjumlahan dan pengurangan multi-langkah melacak perubahan.

Solution:

  • Start: 0 people.
  • Stop 1: 10 adults get on. Total = $0 + 10 = 10$ people.
  • Stop 2: 3 adults get off (-3), 5 children get on (+5). Change = $-3 + 5 = +2$. Total = $10 + 2 = 12$ people.
  • Stop 3: 2 adults get off (-2), 1 child gets off (-1). Change = $-2 - 1 = -3$. Total = $12 - 3 = 9$ people.

Penyelesaian:

  • Mulai: 0 orang.
  • Pemberhentian 1: 10 dewasa naik. Total = $0 + 10 = 10$ orang.
  • Pemberhentian 2: 3 dewasa turun (-3), 5 anak naik (+5). Perubahan = $-3 + 5 = +2$. Total = $10 + 2 = 12$ orang.
  • Pemberhentian 3: 2 dewasa turun (-2), 1 anak turun (-1). Perubahan = $-2 - 1 = -3$. Total = $12 - 3 = 9$ orang.
Answer: There are 9 people on the bus now. / Jawaban: Ada 9 orang di bus sekarang.

Solutions Part 2: Patterns (Number & Counting Focus)

Solution 16 / Pembahasan 16:

Concept: Identifying a growing pattern where elements repeat and increase in count.

Konsep: Mengidentifikasi pola bertumbuh di mana elemen berulang dan bertambah jumlahnya.

Solution:

  • Step 1: Look at the pattern: A, B, AA, BB, AAA, BBB, ...
  • Step 2: The pattern alternates between A's and B's. The number of A's or B's increases by one each time: 1 A, 1 B, 2 A's, 2 B's, 3 A's, 3 B's.
  • Step 3: The last group shown is BBB (3 B's).
  • Step 4: The next group should be A's, and the count should increase to 4: AAAA.
  • Step 5: The group after that should be B's, with a count of 4: BBBB.

Penyelesaian:
