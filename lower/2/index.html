Solution for Question 1

Concept: This is a "combining" problem. We are combining two groups into one.

Solution Steps:

  1. Pictorial: Imagine 7 toy cars in one hand. Then, imagine 4 toy cars in the other hand.
  2. Abstract (Counting): We need to find the total. We start from 7 and count forward 4 steps: 8, 9, 10, 11.
  3. Equation: $7 + 4 = 11$.
Final Answer: Ali has 11 toy cars.
