(EN) Step 2 (Figure 2): We can see that Figure 2 is composed of 4 identical copies of Figure 1 arranged in a row.
(EN) Step 3: Total cubes = $4 \text{ (copies)} \times 4 \text{ (cubes per copy)} = 16$ cubes.

(EN) Step 3: Total cubes = $12 \text{ (bottom)} + 2 \text{ (top)} = 14$ cubes.

(ID) Langkah 2: 2 Galon = 4 Cangkir + 1 Botol. Ganti Botol: 2 Galon = 4 Cangkir + (2 Cangkir) = 6 Cangkir.
(ID) Langkah 3: Temukan 1 Galon. 1 Galon = $6 \text{ Cangkir} \div 2 = 3$ Cangkir.
(ID) Langkah 4: 1 Ember = 1 Galon + 1 Cangkir + 1 Botol.
(ID) Langkah 5: Ganti semua unit ke Cangkir. 1 Ember = (3 Cangkir) + 1 Cangkir + (2 Cangkir) = 6 Cangkir.

(EN) Step 1: 1 Bottle = 2 Cups.
(EN) Step 2: 2 Gallons = 4 Cups + 1 Bottle. Substitute Bottle: 2 Gallons = 4 Cups + (2 Cups) = 6 Cups.
(EN) Step 3: Find 1 Gallon. 1 Gallon = $6 \text{ Cups} \div 2 = 3$ Cups.
(EN) Step 4: 1 Pail = 1 Gallon + 1 Cup + 1 Bottle.
(EN) Step 5: Substitute all units to Cups. 1 Pail = (3 Cups) + 1 Cup + (2 Cups) = 6 Cups.
