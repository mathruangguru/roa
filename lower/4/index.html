(EN) Step 1 (Bottom Layer): Count all cubes on the floor. It is a 4x3 grid with one corner missing. $4 \times 3 - 1 = 11$ cubes.
(EN) Step 2 (Middle Layer): Count all cubes on the 2nd floor. We can see 5 cubes.
(EN) Step 3 (Top Layer): Count all cubes on the 3rd floor. We can see 2 cubes.
(EN) Step 4: Total cubes = $11 \text{ (bottom)} + 5 \text{ (middle)} + 2 \text{ (top)} = 18$ cubes.
