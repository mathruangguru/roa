1. Solution / Pembahasan

(EN) First, let's analyze the number $114$. We break it into its factors: $114 = 2 \times 57 = 2 \times 3 \times 19 = 3 \times 38$. We are told the Highest Common Factor (HCF) of $4AB$ (a 3-digit number starting with 4) and $114$ is exactly $38$.
Since the HCF is 38, our number $4AB$ must be divisible by 38. However, it must not be divisible by 3. Why? Because $114$ is divisible by 3 ($114 = 3 \times 38$). If $4AB$ were also divisible by 3, then their common factor would be at least $3 \times 38 = 114$, which contradicts the fact that HCF is only 38.
Let's list multiples of 38 in the 400s:
- $38 \times 10 = 380$ (Not in 400s)
- $38 \times 11 = 418$. Sum of digits = $4+1+8 = 13$. (13 is not div by 3, so this works!). Here $A=1, B=8$, $A+B=9$.
- $38 \times 12 = 456$. Sum of digits = $4+5+6 = 15$. (15 is div by 3, so reject this).
- $38 \times 13 = 494$. Sum of digits = $4+9+4 = 17$. (17 is not div by 3, so this works!). Here $A=9, B=4$, $A+B=13$.
The possible sums ($A+B$) are 9 and 13.
Difference = $13 - 9 = 4$.

(ID) Pertama, mari kita bedah angka $114$. Faktornya adalah: $114 = 2 \times 57 = 2 \times 3 \times 19 = 3 \times 38$. Soal memberitahu kita bahwa Faktor Persekutuan Terbesar (FPB) antara $4AB$ dan $114$ adalah tepat $38$.
Artinya, bilangan $4AB$ harus bisa dibagi 38. TAPI, bilangan itu tidak boleh bisa dibagi 3. Kenapa? Karena $114$ itu kelipatan 3 ($114 = 3 \times 38$). Jika $4AB$ juga kelipatan 3, maka faktor bersamanya akan menjadi $3 \times 38 = 114$, padahal soal bilang FPB-nya cuma 38.
Mari kita daftar kelipatan 38 di angka 400-an:
- $38 \times 11 = 418$. Jumlah digit = $4+1+8=13$. (13 tidak bisa dibagi 3, jadi ini BISA). Di sini $A=1, B=8 \rightarrow A+B=9$.
- $38 \times 12 = 456$. Jumlah digit = $4+5+6=15$. (15 bisa dibagi 3, jadi ini TIDAK BOLEH).
- $38 \times 13 = 494$. Jumlah digit = $4+9+4=17$. (17 tidak bisa dibagi 3, jadi ini BISA). Di sini $A=9, B=4 \rightarrow A+B=13$.
Nilai $A+B$ yang mungkin adalah 9 dan 13. Selisihnya adalah $13 - 9 = 4$.

Answer: 4

2. Solution / Pembahasan

(EN) The number of trailing zeros in $n!$ is determined by how many pairs of prime factors (2 and 5) exist in the multiplication $1 \times 2 \times \dots \times n$. Since there are plenty of 2s, we only need to count the factors of 5.
- For $n=1$ to $4$, zeros = 0.
- At $n=5$, we get one factor of 5, so zeros = 1.
- The count stays 1 until $n=9$.
- At $n=10$, we get another 5, so zeros = 2.
- The pattern usually adds 1 zero every time we pass a multiple of 5.
- HOWEVER, at $n=25$, we hit $25 = 5 \times 5$. This adds two 5s at once. So the zero count jumps by 2 (e.g., from 4 zeros to 6 zeros). It skips the number 5.
Let's list the jumps:
At 5 (count 0 $\to$ 1), at 10 (1 $\to$ 2), at 15 (2 $\to$ 3), at 20 (3 $\to$ 4), at 25 (4 $\to$ 6, skipping 5), at 30 (6 $\to$ 7), at 35 (7 $\to$ 8), at 40 (8 $\to$ 9), at 45 (9 $\to$ 10), at 50 (10 $\to$ 12, skipping 11)...
The numbers skipped (impossible zero counts) are: 5 (skipped at 25), 11 (skipped at 50), 17 (skipped at 75), 23 (skipped at 100), 29 (skipped at 125, actually at 125 jump is 3, so it skips 28 $\to$ 31, skipping 29, 30).
Let's re-check carefully for numbers up to 40:
- Skips occur at multiples of 25 (jump 2), 125 (jump 3).
- Jump at 25: skip 5.
- Jump at 50: skip 11.
- Jump at 75: skip 17.
- Jump at 100: skip 23.
- Jump at 125: count goes from 28 (at 124) to 31 (at 125). Skips 29, 30.
- Jump at 150: skip 36.
Wait, the question asks "integers from 1 to 40". Let's list the impossible counts $\le 40$:
5, 11, 17, 23, 29, 30 (from 125), 35? No, let's check jump at 150. $150 = 25 \times 6$. Jump is 2. Count goes from 35? Let's calculate exactly.
$n=124$: floor(124/5)+floor(124/25) = 24+4 = 28.
$n=125$: 125/5 + 125/25 + 125/125 = 25+5+1 = 31. (Skips 29, 30).
$n=149$: 29+5+1 = 35? No. 149/5=29. 149/25=5. 149/125=1. Sum=35.
$n=150$: 30+6+1 = 37. (Skips 36).
So missing numbers: 5, 11, 17, 23, 29, 30, 36 (wait, not 35). Wait, next jump is at 175 (skips 41).
Correct list under 40: 5, 11, 17, 23, 29, 30 (special case 125), 36 (from 150? No, wait. 35 $\to$ 37 skips 36).
Let's re-verify jump at 125. $124!$ has 28 zeros. $125!$ has 31 zeros. Missing: 29, 30.
Jump at 150 ($25 \times 6$). $149!$ has 35 zeros. $150!$ has $35+2=37$ zeros (adds factor 150 which is $2 \times 75 = 2 \times 3 \times 25$, two 5s). So it skips 36.
List: 5, 11, 17, 23, 29, 30, 36. Total 7 numbers.

(ID) Jumlah angka nol di belakang $n!$ bergantung pada berapa banyak angka 5 dalam perkaliannya (karena angka 2 pasti banyak). Setiap ketemu kelipatan 5, jumlah nol nambah 1. Tapi, setiap ketemu kelipatan 25 ($5 \times 5$), jumlah nol nambah 2 sekaligus (meloncat).
Mari kita lihat angka nol yang "terlompati":
- Saat $n=24 \to 25$: Nol naik dari 4 menjadi 6. (Angka 5 terlewati).
- Saat $n=49 \to 50$: Nol naik dari 10 menjadi 12. (Angka 11 terlewati).
- Saat $n=74 \to 75$: Nol naik dari 16 menjadi 18. (Angka 17 terlewati).
- Saat $n=99 \to 100$: Nol naik dari 22 menjadi 24. (Angka 23 terlewati).
- Saat $n=124 \to 125$ ($125=5^3$): Nol naik dari 28 menjadi 31 (nambah 3). (Angka 29 dan 30 terlewati).
- Saat $n=149 \to 150$: Nol naik dari 35 menjadi 37. (Angka 36 terlewati).
Angka yang tidak mungkin: 5, 11, 17, 23, 29, 30, 36. Totalnya ada 7 bilangan.

3. Solution / Pembahasan

(EN) Let dimensions be $L, W, H$.
Given: $LWH + (LW+WH+HL) + (L+W+H) = 2024$.
Add 1 to both sides to factorize: $LWH + (LW+WH+HL) + (L+W+H) + 1 = 2025$.
This factors beautifully into $(L+1)(W+1)(H+1) = 2025$.
We need to find factors of $2025 = 5^2 \times 9^2 = 3^4 \times 5^2$.
Constraints: Dimensions are integers $>5$, so $(L+1), (W+1), (H+1)$ must be integers $>6$.
Possible sets of 3 factors for 2025 where each > 6:
1. $9 \times 15 \times 15 = 2025$. (Dimensions: $8, 14, 14$). Diagonal $x = \sqrt{8^2 + 14^2 + 14^2} = \sqrt{64+196+196} = \sqrt{456} \approx 21.35$. Floor $[x] = 21$.
2. $9 \times 9 \times 25 = 2025$. (Dimensions: $8, 8, 24$). Diagonal $x = \sqrt{8^2 + 8^2 + 24^2} = \sqrt{64+64+576} = \sqrt{704} \approx 26.53$. Floor $[x] = 26$.
Sum of values = $21 + 26 = 47$.

(ID) Misalkan panjang, lebar, tinggi adalah $P, L, T$.
Rumus soal: Volume + 1/2 Luas Permukaan + Keliling/4 = 2024. (Sebenarnya rumusnya $P \cdot L \cdot T + (P \cdot L + L \cdot T + P \cdot T) + (P+L+T) = 2024$).
Trik Matematika: Tambahkan 1 di kedua ruas.
$(P \cdot L \cdot T + P \cdot L + L \cdot T + P \cdot T + P + L + T + 1) = 2025$.
Ini bisa difaktorkan menjadi: $(P+1)(L+1)(T+1) = 2025$.
Kita cari 3 angka yang dikalikan hasilnya 2025 ($2025 = 25 \times 81 = 5^2 \times 9^2$). Syarat: karena dimensi $>5$, maka faktornya harus $>6$.
Kemungkinan:
1. $9, 15, 15$. (Sisi aslinya $8, 14, 14$). Diagonal = $\sqrt{8^2+14^2+14^2} = \sqrt{456} \approx 21,3$. Nilai bulat bawah $[x] = 21$.

Jumlah semua nilai yang mungkin = $21 + 26 = 47$.

4. Solution / Pembahasan

(EN) Factorize using $a^2 - b^2 = (a-b)(a+b)$.
$91^4 - 1 = (91^2 - 1)(91^2 + 1)$.
Let's check divisibility. Look at the term $91^2 + 1$.
Notice that $91 = 101 - 10$. Or simply modulo 101 arithmetic: $91 \equiv -10 \pmod{101}$.
So $91^2 + 1 \equiv (-10)^2 + 1 \equiv 100 + 1 \equiv 101 \equiv 0 \pmod{101}$.
This means $91^2 + 1$ is divisible by $101$.
Now look at the options. 2020 is $20 \times 101$.
We know $91^2 - 1 = (91-1)(91+1) = 90 \times 92$. 90 is divisible by 10 and 20.
So the full number is divisible by $101$ and by $20$, so it is divisible by $20 \times 101 = 2020$.

(ID) Gunakan rumus selisih kuadrat: $a^2 - b^2$.
$91^4 - 1 = (91^2 - 1)(91^2 + 1)$.
Kita cek bagian $91^2 + 1$. Coba bagi dengan 101.
$91$ itu sama dengan $-10$ jika dalam modulo 101 (karena $91 - 101 = -10$).
Maka $91^2 + 1 \equiv (-10)^2 + 1 = 100 + 1 = 101$, yang habis dibagi 101.
Jadi faktornya memuat angka 101.
Bagian kirinya $(91^2 - 1) = 90 \times 92$. Angka 90 habis dibagi 20 (atau 10).
Jadi bilangan ini habis dibagi $101 \times 20 = 2020$.

Answer: 2020

5. Solution / Pembahasan

(EN) Given equation: $a + 2b + 3c = 0$.
We can rearrange this to substitute into the brackets:
1. $a + 2b = -3c$
2. $2b + 3c = -a$
3. $a + 3c = -2b$
The expression asks for: $(a+2b)(2b+3c)(a+3c) + 6abc$.
Substitute the values we found: $(-3c) \times (-a) \times (-2b) + 6abc$.
Multiply the first part: $(-3)(-1)(-2) \times (c)(a)(b) = -6abc$.
Total: $-6abc + 6abc = 0$.

(ID) Persamaan awal: $a + 2b + 3c = 0$.
Kita ubah bentuknya untuk mengganti isi kurung:
1. $a + 2b = -3c$ (pindah $3c$ ke kanan)
2. $2b + 3c = -a$ (pindah $a$ ke kanan)
3. $a + 3c = -2b$ (pindah $2b$ ke kanan)
Soal meminta: $(a+2b)(2b+3c)(a+3c) + 6abc$.
