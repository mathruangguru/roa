1. Solution / Pembahasan
(EN) $114 = 38 \times 3$. Since HCF is 38, $4AB$ must be a multiple of 38 but NOT a multiple of 3. Multiples of 38 in 400s: 418 (sum=13, not div by 3, OK), 456 (sum=15, div by 3, NO), 494 (sum=17, not div by 3, OK). $A+B$ values are $1+8=9$ and $9+4=13$. Diff = $13-9=4$.
(ID) $114 = 38 \times 3$. Karena FPB adalah 38, $4AB$ harus kelipatan 38 tapi BUKAN kelipatan 3. Kelipatan 38 di 400-an: 418 (jumlah digit 13, ok), 456 (jumlah 15, habis dibagi 3, tolak), 494 (jumlah 17, ok). Nilai $A+B$ adalah 9 dan 13. Selisih = 4.
Answer: 4
