2. Solution:

(EN) The number of trailing zeros in $n!$ is determined by Legendre's Formula (counting factors of 5). The count jumps when $n$ is a multiple of 5. It jumps by 2 when $n$ is a multiple of 25.
For $n=24$, zeros = $\lfloor 24/5 \rfloor = 4$.
For $n=25$, zeros = $\lfloor 25/5 \rfloor + \lfloor 25/25 \rfloor = 5+1 = 6$.
There is no $n!$ with 5 zeros. This happens at multiples of 25.
Missing counts in range 1-40: 5, 11, 17, 23, 29, 30 (at 125, jump is larger), 35, 36? No, just multiples of 6: 5, 11, 17, 23, 29, 35. Wait, check 30. $124! \to 24+4=28$ zeros. $125! \to 25+5+1=31$ zeros. So 29 and 30 are skipped.
In range 1-40: 5, 11, 17, 23, 29, 30, 35. Total 7 numbers.

(ID) Jumlah nol di akhir $n!$ ditentukan oleh banyaknya faktor 5. Jumlah ini melompat saat $n$ kelipatan 5, dan melompat ekstra saat $n$ kelipatan 25.
Pada $n=24$, nol = 4. Pada $n=25$, nol = 6. (5 terlewati).
Pola terlewati: 5, 11, 17, 23, 29, 30 (karena di 125 melompat 3 angka: 28 ke 31), 35.
