1. Solution / Pembahasan

Concept: Factors & Multiples (Faktor & Kelipatan)

(EN) The number $114$ can be factored into $114 = 3 \times 38$. The Highest Common Factor (HCF) of $4AB$ and $114$ is $38$. This implies two conditions: 1) $4AB$ must be divisible by 38, and 2) $4AB$ must NOT be divisible by 3 (otherwise the HCF would be $3 \times 38 = 114$). Let's check multiples of 38 in the 400s: $38 \times 10 = 380$ (too small), $38 \times 11 = 418$. Sum of digits for 418 is $4+1+8=13$. Since 13 is not divisible by 3, this number is valid ($A=1, B=8, A+B=9$). Next, $38 \times 12 = 456$. Sum of digits is 15, which is divisible by 3, so we reject it. Next, $38 \times 13 = 494$. Sum of digits is 17, not divisible by 3, so valid ($A=9, B=4, A+B=13$). The maximum sum is 13, minimum is 9. The difference is $13 - 9 = 4$.

(ID) Bilangan $114$ difaktorkan menjadi $114 = 3 \times 38$. Diketahui FPB dari bilangan $4AB$ dan $114$ adalah 38. Ini berarti dua hal: 1) $4AB$ harus habis dibagi 38, dan 2) $4AB$ TIDAK boleh habis dibagi 3 (jika habis dibagi 3, maka FPB-nya menjadi $3 \times 38 = 114$, bukan 38). Mari kita cari kelipatan 38 di angka 400-an: $38 \times 11 = 418$. Jumlah angkanya $4+1+8=13$. Karena 13 tidak habis dibagi 3, angka ini valid ($A=1, B=8, A+B=9$). Selanjutnya, $38 \times 12 = 456$. Jumlah angkanya 15 (habis dibagi 3), jadi ditolak. Selanjutnya, $38 \times 13 = 494$. Jumlah angkanya 17 (tidak habis dibagi 3), valid ($A=9, B=4, A+B=13$). Nilai maksimum $A+B$ adalah 13, minimum 9. Selisihnya $13 - 9 = 4$.

Answer: 4

2. Solution / Pembahasan

Concept: Trailing Zeros (Legendre's Formula)

(EN) The number of trailing zeros in $n!$ is determined by the number of prime factors of 5 in $n!$ (since factors of 2 are abundant). Normally, the count of zeros increases by 1 at every multiple of 5. However, at multiples of 25 ($5^2$), the count increases by 2 because 25 contributes two 5s. Similarly, at 125, it increases by 3. When the count jumps by 2, one integer value is skipped. When it jumps by 3, two values are skipped. We check for skips up to $n=40$: At $n=25$, zero count jumps from 4 (for $n=24$) to 6. So, 5 is skipped. At $n=50$, count jumps from 10 to 12 (skip 11). At $n=75$, skip 17. At $n=100$, skip 23. At $n=125$, skip 29 and 30. At $n=150$, skip 36. The "impossible" numbers under 40 are: 5, 11, 17, 23, 29, 30, 36. There are 7 such numbers.

(ID) Jumlah nol di akhir $n!$ ditentukan oleh banyaknya faktor 5. Biasanya, jumlah nol bertambah 1 setiap kelipatan 5. Namun, pada kelipatan 25 ($5^2$), jumlah nol bertambah 2 sekaligus (karena 25 menyumbang dua angka 5). Pada kelipatan 125, bertambah 3. Angka yang "terlewati" saat lonjakan ini adalah angka yang tidak mungkin menjadi jumlah nol. Mari daftar angka yang terlewati: Saat $n=25$, nol naik dari 4 ke 6 (angka 5 terlewat). Saat $n=50$, nol naik dari 10 ke 12 (angka 11 terlewat). Saat $n=75$, angka 17 terlewat. Saat $n=100$, angka 23 terlewat. Saat $n=125$, angka 29 dan 30 terlewat. Saat $n=150$, angka 36 terlewat. Jadi, angka yang tidak mungkin di bawah 40 adalah: 5, 11, 17, 23, 29, 30, 36. Total ada 7 angka.

Answer: 7

3. Solution / Pembahasan

Concept: Algebraic Factorization

(EN) Let dimensions be $L, W, H$. The problem gives $LWH + (LW+WH+HL) + (L+W+H) = 2024$. Add 1 to both sides to factorize: $LWH + LW + WH + HL + L + W + H + 1 = 2025$. This simplifies to $(L+1)(W+1)(H+1) = 2025$. We need to find 3 factors of $2025$ ($2025 = 3^4 \times 5^2$). Since dimensions $>5$, factors must be $>6$. Possible sets: 1) $9, 15, 15$ (giving sides 8, 14, 14). Diagonal $x = \sqrt{8^2+14^2+14^2} \approx 21.35$, so $[x]=21$. 2) $9, 9, 25$ (giving sides 8, 8, 24). Diagonal $x = \sqrt{8^2+8^2+24^2} \approx 26.53$, so $[x]=26$. Total sum = $21+26=47$.

(ID) Misalkan dimensi $P, L, T$. Persamaan soal: $P \cdot L \cdot T + (P \cdot L + L \cdot T + P \cdot T) + (P+L+T) = 2024$. Tambahkan 1 di kedua ruas agar bisa difaktorkan: $(P+1)(L+1)(T+1) = 2025$. Kita cari 3 faktor dari 2025 yang masing-masing $>6$. Kemungkinan: 1) $9, 15, 15$ (Sisi: 8, 14, 14). Diagonal $\approx 21,3$. Nilai bulat [x]=21. 2) $9, 9, 25$ (Sisi: 8, 8, 24). Diagonal $\approx 26,5$. Nilai bulat [x]=26. Total jumlah = $21 + 26 = 47$.

Answer: 47

4. Solution / Pembahasan

Concept: Difference of Squares & Modular Arithmetic

(EN) Factorize $91^4 - 1$ as $(91^2 - 1)(91^2 + 1)$. Let's check the term $91^2 + 1$. Notice that $91 = 101 - 10$, so $91 \equiv -10 \pmod{101}$. Thus, $91^2 + 1 \equiv (-10)^2 + 1 \equiv 100 + 1 \equiv 101 \equiv 0 \pmod{101}$. This means $91^2 + 1$ is divisible by 101. Also, $91^2 - 1$ is divisible by 20 (since $91^2$ ends in 1). Therefore, the entire expression is divisible by $20 \times 101 = 2020$.

(ID) Faktorkan $91^4 - 1$ menjadi $(91^2 - 1)(91^2 + 1)$. Cek bagian $91^2 + 1$. Dalam modulo 101, $91 \equiv -10$. Maka $91^2 + 1 \equiv (-10)^2 + 1 = 101 \equiv 0$. Artinya habis dibagi 101. Karena bagian kiri $(91^2 - 1)$ habis dibagi 20, maka bilangan ini habis dibagi $20 \times 101 = 2020$.

Answer: 2020

Concept: Algebraic Manipulation

(EN) We know $a^2+b^2+c^2 = 4$. Rewrite the numerators: $a^2+b^2 = 4-c^2$, etc. The first fraction becomes $\frac{4-c^2}{2-c} = \frac{(2-c)(2+c)}{2-c} = 2+c$. Similarly, the others become $2+a$ and $2+b$. Summing them: $(2+c)+(2+a)+(2+b) = 6 + (a+b+c)$. Given $a+b+c=3$, the result is $6+3=9$.

(ID) Diketahui $a^2+b^2+c^2 = 4$. Ubah pembilang: $a^2+b^2 = 4-c^2$. Pecahan pertama menjadi $\frac{4-c^2}{2-c} = 2+c$. Pecahan lain menjadi $2+a$ dan $2+b$. Jumlah total = $6 + (a+b+c)$. Karena $a+b+c=3$, hasilnya $6+3=9$.

Answer: 9

Concept: Polynomial Evaluation

(EN) Let $x=2020$, so $2021 = x+1$. Rewrite the polynomial: $P(x) = x^5 - (x+1)x^4 + (x+1)x^3 - \dots - 1$. Expanding terms shows they cancel out telescopically: $x^5 - x^5 - x^4 + x^4 \dots$ leaving only $x - 1$. So $P(2020) = 2020 - 1 = 2019$.

(ID) Misalkan $x=2020$, maka $2021=x+1$. Tulis ulang polinom dengan $(x+1)$. Suku-suku akan saling menghilangkan secara teleskopik, menyisakan $x-1$. Hasilnya $2020-1=2019$.
