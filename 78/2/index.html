5. Solution:

Expansion of $(3 + x - 2x^2)^4$. We need $x^3$. Let the terms be $A=3, B=x, C=-2x^2$. We choose terms from 4 brackets that multiply to $x^3$.
Penjabaran dari $(3 + x - 2x^2)^4$. Kita mencari $x^3$. Misalkan suku-sukunya $A=3, B=x, C=-2x^2$. Kita pilih suku dari 4 kurung yang jika dikalikan menghasilkan $x^3$.

Possible combinations of $(A, B, C)$ powers:
Kemungkinan kombinasi pangkat $(A, B, C)$:

1. $B^3 \cdot A^1$: Choose $x$ three times, $3$ once. Ways: $\binom{4}{3} = 4$. Value: $4 \times (x)^3 \times (3)^1 = 12x^3$.
1. $B^3 \cdot A^1$: Pilih $x$ tiga kali, $3$ sekali. Cara: $\binom{4}{3} = 4$. Nilai: $4 \times (x)^3 \times (3)^1 = 12x^3$.

2. $B^1 \cdot C^1 \cdot A^2$: Choose $x$ once, $-2x^2$ once, $3$ twice. Ways: $4! / (1!1!2!) = 12$. Value: $12 \times (x) \times (-2x^2) \times (3)^2 = 12 \times -2 \times 9 x^3 = -216x^3$.
2. $B^1 \cdot C^1 \cdot A^2$: Pilih $x$ sekali, $-2x^2$ sekali, $3$ dua kali. Cara: $4! / (1!1!2!) = 12$. Nilai: $12 \times (x) \times (-2x^2) \times (3)^2 = 12 \times -2 \times 9 x^3 = -216x^3$.

Total coeff: $12 - 216 = -204$.
Total koefisien: $12 - 216 = -204$.

Answer / Jawaban: -204
